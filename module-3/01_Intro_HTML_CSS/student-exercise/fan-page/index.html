About

Joe graduated from NKU in 2005 and has been building applications ever since. From command line scripts to enterprise CRM's, he has seen it all! Most recently, he was on the implementation team for local startup building applications for iOS, Android and Raspberry Pi. He has experience with a multitude of languages, and enjoys the process of creating new and innovative solutions with code.

Joe enjoys mentoring developers and seeing them reach their full potential. He has tutored first graders at Rothenberg elementary, and also taught programming classes as a 2nd career at Amazon Facilities employees. As a life-long learner, he regularly attends Meetups and enjoys learning about new trends in technology.
